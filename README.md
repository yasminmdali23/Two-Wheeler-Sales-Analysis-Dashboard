# Two-Wheeler Sales Performance Dashboard 📊🏍️

An interactive, multi-page Power BI dashboard designed to evaluate operational revenue, consumer preferences, product volumes, and geographic distribution trends across major two-wheeler segments.

## 📈 Key Metrics & Insights Delivered
* **Revenue Dynamics:** Evaluated a data portfolio representing **$188M in total sales revenue**, driving **$45M in gross profit margins** against **$143M in production cost values**.
* **Volume & Performance:** Tracked an active volume of **1.5K total orders** across product segments, securing a strong overall customer rating average of **4.26 out of 5 stars**.
* **Operational Expenses:** Mapped **$103M in financed allocations** alongside an evaluated total of **$8M in asset insurance coverage amounts**.

---

## 🛠️ Dashboard Architecture & Key Features
The repository contains a multi-page `.pbix` report engineered with clean user-experience standards:
1. **Home Landing Page:** A minimalist, sleek entry portal showcasing the Honda CB350 with integrated custom button links to navigate right into the active analytical data modules.
2. **Main Performance Sheet:** Built with customized line charts for monthly trends, state-level ring charts, and target KPI cards tracking active business financial conditions.
3. **Detailed Data Repository Matrix:** A full backend reporting sheet utilizing model-specific and city-wide slicers to instantly isolate local dealer transactions across major urban hubs.
